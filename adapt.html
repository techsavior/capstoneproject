Adapting To New Technology



  • Adapting to new technologies goes hand in hand with the digital divide. Once you gain access to technology you need to learn how to be proficient with it to keep safe. Learning what sources to find credible, what websites to avoid, and what files are suspicious can help save you a trip to the store and money. Ways to adapt include, reading about new technology through news articles or forums. But make sure the sources you are searching through are credible first. Another way is watching YouTube videos. There are a plethora of YouTube channels out there that offer tech news in some way. Adapting to technology isn't easy but its always important to take the initiative and learn.
