Why is Digital Literacy So Important?

In a world where technology has become evermore important, navigating the vast world of information within technology in a safe and easy way has become even more important. One big reason digital literacy is so important is safety. According to Samuel Levine, Director of the FTC’s Bureau of Consumer Protection “Digital tools are making it easier than ever to target hard-working Americans, and we see the effects of that in the data we're releasing today” (Federal Trade Commission, 2024). Technology can be useful but also harmful, on one hand you can use technology to further your knowledge and even make money. But the same can be said about the harm that can be caused, such as loss of money, loss of personal information, and in rare cases even loss in life.
